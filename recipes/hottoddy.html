Hot Toddy

It's another perfect proof that you only need three ingredients to be happy

Ok, maybe its four ingredients, but who really counts water anyway? This will make you very happy if you're into that sort of thing and being able to articulate has always bothered you.

Ingredients

  • Whisk(e)y, i like a Speyside Scotch
  • Honey
  • 1 Lemon

Steps

  1. Put 2 cm of Whisk(e)y in a glass or mug.
  2. Add a spoon full of honey and squeeze in some lemon.
  3. Fill it up with hot water.
  4. Drink it.
  5. Repeat.
